<h2>Symptoms of Depression</h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Fatigue" src="images/fatigue-icon.svg">
</div>
<p><strong>Fatigue:</strong> Extreme tiredness, making daily tasks difficult.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Lack of Motivation" src="images/motivation-icon.svg">
</div>
<p><strong>Lack of Motivation:</strong> Difficulty engaging in enjoyable activities.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Sleep Changes" src="images/sleep-icon.svg">
</div>
<p><strong>Sleep Changes:</strong> Insomnia or excessive sleeping.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Appetite Changes" src="images/appetite-icon.svg">
</div>
<p><strong>Appetite Changes:</strong> Significant weight loss or gain.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Self-Perception Issues" src="images/self-perception-icon.svg">
</div>
<p><strong>Self-Perception Issues:</strong> Negative thoughts about oneself.</p>
<h2>Causes of Depression</h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Stress" src="images/stress-icon.svg">
</div>
<p><strong>Stress:</strong> Life events can trigger or worsen depression.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Genetics" src="images/dna-icon.svg">
</div>
<p><strong>Genetics:</strong> Family history can increase risk.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Life Experiences" src="images/trauma-icon.svg">
</div>
<p><strong>Life Experiences:</strong> Trauma and loss contribute to depression.</p>
<h2>Support &amp; Recovery</h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Talking to Someone" src="images/support-icon.svg">
</div>
<p><strong>Talking to Someone:</strong> Connecting with loved ones helps.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Therapy Options" src="images/therapy-icon.svg">
</div>
<p><strong>Therapy Options:</strong> Professional counseling like CBT.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Medication" src="images/medication-icon.svg">
</div>
<p><strong>Medication:</strong> Antidepressants may be beneficial.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Lifestyle Changes" src="images/lifestyle-icon.svg">
</div>
<p><strong>Lifestyle Changes:</strong> Exercise, diet, and sleep improvements.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Recovery" src="images/recovery-icon.svg">
</div>
<p><strong>Recovery is Possible:</strong> Support and treatment can improve
mental health.</p>
