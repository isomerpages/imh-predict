<p><strong>Dr Lee Eng Sing</strong> is a Senior Consultant, Family Physician
and Deputy Director of the Clinical Research Unit at National Healthcare
Group Polyclinics. He is also an Assistant Professor (Clinical Practice)
and Director of Primary Care and Family Medicine Programme in Lee Kong
Chian School of Medicine, Nanyang Technological University. Dr Lee contributes
as a Fellow at the MOH Office for Healthcare Transformation and is also
an adjunct faculty in Geriatric Education and Research Institute. Dr Lee
obtained his medical degree from Aberdeen University (Scotland) and then
attained Masters and Fellowship of Family Medicine in Singapore. He completed
his PhD in Family Medicine from Western University (Canada) in 2019. Dr
Lee has published close to 40 articles and is active in building the capacity
and capability of primary care research in Singapore. His research interests
are in multimorbidity, chronic disease management and digital solutions
in primary care. He will ensure the support and resources for the recruitment
of participants as well as data collection longitudinally in the polyclinic
setting.</p>
